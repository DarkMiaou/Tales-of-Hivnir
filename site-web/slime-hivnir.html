Slime
Type : Enemy Mob
Found : Badlands
Health Points : 150
Attack Points : 25

The slime is a gummy blue monster often found in the plains of the Badlands. Don't let his cute looks and blue eyes fool you, he is a formidable opponent on the battlefield. His melee default attack dealing you a quarter of your base health and his blue glu protecting him for 150 attack points will sure be tougth to break throught.
