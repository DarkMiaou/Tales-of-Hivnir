Sky Castle
The Sky Castle, also know as Tutorial is the first map the player appears on when the game starts. It is made so that the player can learn the basic mechanics slowly but surely. Composed of three main area : Dungeon, Tower and Gardens, it is also the home of its unique and only monster : the Log. The dungeon is a big building on which the player spawns on, it has a interior that is completly empty, consequence of an unexpected abandon of the castle, there is also a walkay to find with some valuable loot a the end of the hallway. The Tower, also having been abandonned, was the home of a praying group, hence the installation of worshiping statues and columns, its second floor is composed of multiple chests and storage units. To finsih off, the gardens are the exterior part of the map, living area of the log, they can be treacherous lands to roam on.
